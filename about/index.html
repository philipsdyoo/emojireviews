About

Making food reviews fun and interesting but also hard to read...

Emoji Reviews is my personal blog for reviewing all kinds of products and services, primarily food, in an interesting way. With the use of emoji becoming more and more commonplace, I decided to experiment and challenge myself by communicating to the audience with only emoji. It's proven to be quite difficult, but I include a text version of the review at the bottom of each post.
